rain-now, available at waddlegames.github.io/rain-now provides accurate weather information from the NWS, OpenWeather, PirateWeather, and more, 
including weather and civil alerts, forecasts, and even a minute-by-minute precipitation forecast. My goal is to make a website in the style of a modern weather app, 
but with more accuracy like that of an official NWS website. For any bug reports, feature ideas, and more, please feel free to leave a comment.
